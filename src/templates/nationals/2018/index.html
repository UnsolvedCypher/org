CubingUSA Nationals 2018

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA Nationals 2018 will be held July 27-29, 2018 at the Salt Palace Convention Center in Salt Lake City, Utah!

Registration will be held on the WCA website starting on July 1, 2018 at 3:00 AM PST. All competitors must register online. There will be a limit of 20 competitors, and a registration fee of $5 per competitor. There will be qualifying times in all events; please see the Events page for more details.
