CubingUSA Nationals 2018

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA Nationals 2018 will be held July 27-29, 2018 at the Salt Palace Convention Center in Salt Lake City, Utah!

CubingUSA Nationals 2018 will be the 14th US National Championship. Each year, the competition brings together hundreds of the top American speedcubers, as well as many international competitors, for a fun weekend of solving puzzles.

Nationals is the largest and most professional speedcubing competition in the USA each year, brought to you by a dedicated team of volunteers who work year-round to support our community. This year's Nationals will be the largest speedcubing competition in the USA to date.

Key Facts

Dates: July 27-29, 2018
Location: Salt Lake City, Utah
Venue: Salt Palace Convention Center

Registration

Registration will take place on the WCA website. There are qualifying times in all events; you must qualify in at least one event to register. Spectators do not need to register.
Start: January 9, 6:00 PM MST
Cost: $99 for competitors
Limit: 850 competitors
Qualifying Times: See events page.
You can get a 50% refund for cancelling until the refund deadline, after which we will no longer give refunds.

Key Dates

  • January 9: Registration opens
  • June 1: Cancellation 50% refund deadline
  • June 18: Deadline for qualifying times
  • June 25: Last day to modify registration
  • July 27-29: Competition!
