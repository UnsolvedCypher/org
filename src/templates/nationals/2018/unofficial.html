Saturday Night Schedule

  • 7:15: 3x3 Fewest Moves ends

  • 7:30 – 8:30: Why We Cube

    A documentary by Chris Olson.

  • 8:30 – 9:10: The Sail Challenge hosted by TheCubicle.us

    The top 16 competitors who did not make 3x3 Round 2 will be invited to take part in a head-to-head competition with QiYi Sail 3x3s. Each competitor will receive one unlubed Sail and will have five minutes to prepare it using materials provided (includes lubricants and screwdrivers). After five minutes, all competitors must begin assembling their cubes immediately. Competitors will not be permitted to adjust their cubes after final assembly. Once all the cubes are finished, the competitors will compete head-to-head in a bracket format.

  • 9:10 – 9:45: Head-to-Head Double Elimination 3x3 Tournament

    A head-to-head 3x3 tournament of the top 16 competitors from Round 1 of CubingUSA Nationals.
